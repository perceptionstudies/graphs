Reading a graph is like reading a paragraph

This webpage contains stimuli and demonstrations supporting our paper:

Reading a graph is like reading a paragraph
by Tal Boger & Steven Franconeri. In press, JEP:General.

Below, you will find links to the experiments we performed. Each data vignette contains two experiments; one in which the graph hides the improbable relationship (i.e., makes it harder to notice), and one in which the graph implicitly highlights the improbable relationship.

  • Phone vignette: Hides improbable relationship, highlights improbable relationship
  • Menu vignette: Hides improbable relationship, highlights improbable relationship
  • Age vignette: Hides improbable relationship, highlights improbable relationship
  • Age (2x2, exploratory) vignette: Hides improbable relationship, highlights improbable relationship
